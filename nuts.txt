Almond  A nutrient-dense nut rich in healthy fats, protein, vitamin E, and good for heart health.
Cashew  A creamy, mildly sweet nut rich in healthy fats, magnesium, and commonly used in Indian dishes.
Walnut  A brain-shaped nut high in omega-3 fatty acids and beneficial for brain and heart health.
Pistachio  A small green nut rich in protein, fiber, and antioxidants with a slightly sweet taste.
Peanut  An affordable, protein-rich legume commonly eaten as a nut, good for energy and nutrition.
Hazelnut  A sweet, crunchy nut rich in healthy fats and widely used in chocolates and spreads.
Macadamia  A buttery, rich nut high in healthy fats and calories, often used in desserts.
Pecan  A soft, sweet nut rich in antioxidants and commonly used in baked goods.
Brazil Nut  A large nut extremely rich in selenium, important for thyroid function.
Pine Nut  A small, soft nut used in cooking, rich in healthy fats and essential nutrients.
