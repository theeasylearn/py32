Potato  A versatile root vegetable rich in carbohydrates and commonly used in many Indian dishes.
Tomato  A juicy, slightly tangy vegetable packed with vitamins and widely used in curries and salads.
Onion  A pungent vegetable that adds flavor to almost every Indian recipe and is rich in antioxidants.
Carrot  A crunchy, sweet vegetable high in vitamin A, good for eye health.
Spinach  A leafy green vegetable rich in iron, vitamins, and essential nutrients.
Cauliflower  A white, compact vegetable used in sabzis and curries, rich in fiber and vitamins.
Cabbage  A leafy vegetable used in salads and cooking, known for its digestive benefits.
Brinjal (Eggplant)  A soft, spongy vegetable used in dishes like bharta, rich in fiber.
Ladyfinger (Okra)  A green vegetable known for its slimy texture, rich in fiber and good for digestion.
Green Peas  Small, sweet vegetables packed with protein, fiber, and essential nutrients.
